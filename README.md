# Decentralized Economic Development and Job Creation Network

A comprehensive blockchain-based system for fostering local economic growth through smart contracts that coordinate business incubation, workforce training, impact measurement, procurement preferences, and entrepreneurship education.

## System Overview

This network consists of five independent smart contracts that work together to create a robust economic development ecosystem:

### 1. Small Business Incubation Contract (`business-incubation.clar`)
- Manages business registration and support programs
- Tracks incubation milestones and resource allocation
- Provides funding mechanisms for qualifying businesses
- Maintains business performance metrics

### 2. Workforce Training Coordination Contract (`workforce-training.clar`)
- Coordinates job training programs with employment opportunities
- Matches trainees with appropriate skill development paths
- Tracks training completion and certification
- Connects trained workers with local employers

### 3. Economic Impact Measurement Contract (`economic-impact.clar`)
- Measures and tracks economic development investments
- Calculates ROI on various development programs
- Monitors job creation and business growth metrics
- Provides transparent reporting on program effectiveness

### 4. Local Procurement Preference Contract (`local-procurement.clar`)
- Manages local business certification and verification
- Implements preference scoring for local vendors
- Tracks procurement spending with local businesses
- Provides incentives for buying local

### 5. Entrepreneurship Education Contract (`entrepreneurship-education.clar`)
- Manages business skills training programs
- Coordinates mentorship matching
- Tracks educational progress and achievements
- Provides certification for completed programs

## Key Features

- **Decentralized Governance**: Community-driven decision making
- **Transparent Tracking**: All activities recorded on blockchain
- **Incentive Alignment**: Token rewards for participation and success
- **Local Focus**: Prioritizes local economic development
- **Measurable Impact**: Built-in metrics and reporting

## Contract Interactions

While contracts operate independently, they share common data structures and can reference each other's public data for comprehensive economic development tracking.

## Getting Started

1. Deploy contracts to Stacks blockchain
2. Initialize with local economic development parameters
3. Register businesses, training programs, and educational resources
4. Begin tracking economic activities and impacts
